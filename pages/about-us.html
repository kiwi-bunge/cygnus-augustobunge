Come read our story!

Hi there! My name is Juan Martin, but everyone knows me as "Tato".

All my life I was passionate about building stuff. Through the years after highschool I did different jobs, but it was around 12 years ago when I started to get involved with carpentry.

Although I did carpentry in my home country, Argentina, when I arrived in Australia in 2017 I got impressed by the construction style and culture. Without losing any time I got a job in a Building company.

After a few years of working for someone else I decided to go on my own, to be able fulfill the dreams or projects of the people in my own way and style.

At Cygnus Woodworks we have a top tier carpenters team. You can check our different services and have a look to our achievments.
